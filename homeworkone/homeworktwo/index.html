About Uranus

Uranus is the 7th planet from the sun. It has a weird tilt and is cold. Cool right?

The Moons of Uranus

Uranus has a bunch of moons. Some are big, others are small. They all have interesting features.

Fun Facts

  • Uranus rotates on its side!
  • Some moons have big canyons and craters.
  • Uranus was discovered a long time ago.
