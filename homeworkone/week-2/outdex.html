Description

The Mercedes W20, introduced in 2020, was a dominant Formula 1 car powered by a 1.6L V6 turbo hybrid engine, producing around 1,000 horsepower. With its sleek aerodynamic design and innovative features like the Dual-Axis Steering (DAS) system, it offered exceptional performance and handling. Driven by Lewis Hamilton, the W20 helped Mercedes win both the Drivers' and Constructors' Championships, with Hamilton claiming 11 race victories that season. Known for its speed, reliability, and cutting-edge technology, the W20 became one of the most successful and advanced F1 cars of its time.
