Venus Flytrap

The Venus Flytrap (Dionaea muscipula) is a carnivorous plant native to subtropical wetlands of the East Coast of the United States.

It captures and digests insects and arachnids.

The plant has sensitive trigger hairs inside its lobes that snap shut when touched.

The Venus Flytrap thrives in nutrient-poor, acidic soil.

It can take up to 10 days to digest a single insect.

The plant relies on bright, direct sunlight to grow properly.
